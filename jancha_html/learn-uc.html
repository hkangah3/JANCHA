University College

If University College were any closer to campus, you'd already be in class. Enjoy walk-to-class convenience, fitness amenities, academic support, and community events that keep you connected. UC is the top choice for first-year housing at Prairie View A&M.

UC sits right next to the academic core of PVAMU, giving freshmen unbeatable access to classes, dining, and campus events. You can wake up later, get to class faster, and stay plugged into student life without relying on transportation.
