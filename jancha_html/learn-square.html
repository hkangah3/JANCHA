The Square

The Square provides a balanced mix of convenience, comfort, and affordability for PVAMU students. Located right next to campus, The Square offers apartment-style living with shared common areas, spacious layouts, and a friendly student community. It’s a strong option for students who want more independence than a dorm while still staying close to university life, classes, and resources.

Strong Community Enviornment

The Square is known for its welcoming student atmosphere. Shared living spaces and community areas help residents build friendships, collaborate on coursework, and enjoy a balanced academic and social experience.

Close to Campus

Just minutes from PVAMU, The Square keeps students connected to campus events, classes, dining, and organizations. It’s an ideal choice for students who want the convenience of walking to campus without sacrificing privacy or space.

Spacious Layout

The Square is known for its wide, open common spaces and roomy floor plans. Whether students are studying, cooking, or hanging out with roommates, the larger living rooms and shared areas make the apartment feel open, comfortable, and easy to enjoy with friends.
