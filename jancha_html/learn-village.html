The Village

The Village is designed specifically for sophomores who are ready for more freedom and independence after their first year. With spacious apartment-style layouts, full kitchens, and a calmer atmosphere, The Village gives students the next step up from freshman housing while still keeping them close to the PVAMU campus. It’s a comfortable, balanced environment where sophomores can focus on academics, enjoy a little more privacy, and grow into their own routine.

Real Apartment Living

The Village gives sophomores a true upgrade from freshman housing with full kitchens, spacious living rooms, and apartment-style layouts that feel more like home. It’s the perfect next step for students who want more comfort and independence without moving completely off campus.

freedom with Support

At The Village, students get more independence and fewer restrictions than first-year dorms, but still benefit from having staff and campus resources close by. It’s a balanced environment that lets sophomores grow while still having help available when they need it.

Social but Calm

The Village has a friendly, laid-back atmosphere that makes it easy to meet people without dealing with constant noise or chaos. Shared living spaces encourage connection, while private bedrooms and a quieter setting give students room to recharge and focus.
